# Smart CRM with AI Lead Scoring

## The Problem
A B2B sales team was wasting 60% of their time chasing low-quality leads. Their existing CRM was a passive database that didn't help reps identify which prospects were most likely to close, leading to missed opportunities and a slow sales cycle.

## The Solution
HunterMussel transformed their sales workflow with an **Intelligence Layer** on top of their CRM data.
- **Predictive Lead Scoring:** An AI model analyzes historical conversion data to assign a 'Propensity to Buy' score to every incoming lead.
- **Hyper-Personalized Follow-ups:** LLMs generate draft responses based on the lead's specific industry and past interactions.
- **Sentiment Analysis:** The system flags 'at-risk' deals by analyzing the tone of recent email exchanges.

## The Result
- **50% Increase** in lead-to-close conversion rates.
- **4x Faster** initial follow-up response time.
- **Data-Driven Sales:** Management can now forecast revenue with 85% accuracy.
