# Delivery App Route Optimization

## The Problem
A regional logistics provider was struggling to scale their manual dispatching process. With 50+ drivers and a growing volume of on-demand orders, human dispatchers could no longer account for real-time traffic, vehicle capacity, and optimal route sequencing. This resulted in overlapping routes, high fuel consumption, and delayed deliveries.

## The Solution
HunterMussel implemented an **AI-driven routing engine** based on a custom Genetic Algorithm. 
- **Real-time Integration:** The system connects with traffic APIs to adjust routes every 5 minutes.
- **Predictive Dispatching:** Using historical demand data, the system suggests driver pre-positioning in high-volume zones.
- **Automated Communication:** Integrated an AI layer that handles route updates and ETA notifications via WhatsApp/SMS.

## The Result
- **22% Reduction** in total fuel consumption within 6 months.
- **35% Improvement** in delivery speed (Time-to-Door).
- **Scalability:** The client doubled their order volume without hiring additional dispatchers.
