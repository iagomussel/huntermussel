# Intelligent Scheduling App

## The Problem
A multi-location service business was losing significant revenue due to client 'no-shows' and manual scheduling errors (double bookings). Staff spent over 15 hours a week manually calling clients to confirm appointments.

## The Solution
We developed a **Smart Scheduling Ecosystem** that proactively manages time.
- **Conflict Resolution Engine:** An AI-powered calendar that optimizes gaps between appointments to maximize driver/service provider density.
- **Automated Conversational Reminders:** An AI agent that handles WhatsApp/SMS confirmations, allowing clients to reschedule or cancel using natural language.
- **Dynamic Pricing:** Implemented a system that suggests 'Off-Peak' discounts to fill empty slots in the schedule.

## The Result
- **15% Reduction** in appointment no-shows.
- **10+ Hours/Week** saved per staff member on administrative tasks.
- **Revenue Growth:** Automated filling of empty slots increased monthly revenue by 12%.
