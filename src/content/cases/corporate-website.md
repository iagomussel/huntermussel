# High-Performance Corporate Website

## The Problem
A professional services firm had a slow, monolithic website that was difficult to update and failed to generate leads. Their organic search visibility was non-existent, and the user experience was disjointed across mobile and desktop.

## The Solution
We rebuilt their digital presence from the ground up using a **Modern Tech Stack**.
- **Architecture:** Next.js for lightning-fast page loads and superior SEO performance.
- **Headless CMS:** Integrated Sanity.io, allowing the marketing team to update content globally without developer intervention.
- **AI Lead Magnet:** Implemented an intelligent chatbot that qualifies visitors and books meetings directly into the team's calendar.

## The Result
- **200% Increase** in organic search traffic within the first quarter.
- **30% More Leads** captured through the AI-integrated interface.
- **Performance:** Achieved a perfect 100/100 Google Lighthouse score for speed and accessibility.
