# AI-Powered LMS

## The Problem
An online education platform faced a common industry challenge: low course completion rates (under 12%). Students often felt overwhelmed by content that was either too easy or too difficult, leading to disengagement and churn.

## The Solution
We built an **Adaptive Learning Engine** that tailors the educational journey to each individual student.
- **Dynamic Assessment:** AI analyzes student quiz performance in real-time to skip mastered topics or provide remedial content.
- **RAG-Powered Tutor:** Integrated a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot that answers student questions 24/7 using the course's specific knowledge base.
- **Automated Grading:** Used LLMs to provide instant, detailed feedback on open-ended essays, reducing manual grading time by 90%.

## The Result
- **40% Increase** in course completion rates.
- **90% Reduction** in administrative overhead for grading and student support.
- **Higher Satisfaction:** Student NPS scores improved by 25 points.
